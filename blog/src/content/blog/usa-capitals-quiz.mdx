How well do you know your US geography? This interactive quiz highlights a state on the map and challenges you to name both the state and its capital city.

**How to play:**
- Select a region to focus on, or quiz yourself on all 50 states
- A state will be highlighted in blue on the map
- Type the state name in the first field
- Type its capital city in the second field
- Press Enter or click Submit to check your answer
- Track your score and try to build a streak!

You can also click any state on the map to quiz yourself on it directly.
