## Overview

Live Scoring is a volleyball scoring application designed to make match tracking simple and accessible. Whether you're a coach, referee, or fan, Live Scoring provides an intuitive interface for keeping score during matches.

**Free to use with no ads.**

## Features

### Easy Score Tracking
- Simple tap-to-score interface
- Support for all volleyball formats (rally scoring, side-out)
- Customizable set and point limits

### Match History
- Complete history of scored matches
- Review past games and statistics

### Offline Support
- Score matches without an internet connection
- Works anywhere, anytime

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I start a new match?**
Tap the "New Match" button on the home screen, enter the team names, and select your scoring format.

**Is the app really free?**
Yes! Live Scoring is completely free with no ads.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, you can score matches without an internet connection.

**Can I use it on my computer?**
Yes, visit [scoring.netmetrics.cloud](https://scoring.netmetrics.cloud) to use the web version as a PWA.

**How do I report a bug or request a feature?**
Email us at evan@netmetrics.cloud with details about your issue or suggestion.

## Privacy

Live Scoring respects your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the scoring service. No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.
