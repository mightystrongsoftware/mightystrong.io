Can you identify US states just by looking at their shape? This quiz shows you a zoomed-in silhouette of a randomly selected state and challenges you to name it.

**How to play:**
- A state silhouette is shown in the preview box
- Type your answer and press Enter or click Submit
- Use the Hint button if you're stuck (reveals first letter, then length, then partial name)
- Small states like Rhode Island are zoomed in so you can see them clearly
- Click any state on the reference map to quiz yourself on it
